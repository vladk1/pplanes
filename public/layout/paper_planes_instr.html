1) As you can see in the right, you have 2 types of planes from which you can choose. The Basic Dart has a benefit of 28 points/meter associated, while The Stable has 25 points/meter.

2) Both planes have a cost of 1 point/second.

3) Comparing the how-to-build pictograms, you need to make a decision of which one you want to play.

4) You are interested in increasing your score, therefore you'd want to maximize the Net Benefit.

Net Benefit = Benefit - Cost

5) Build the plane and measure your building time.

6) Fly the plane and measure the flying distance.
