Paper Planes
     or decision making under uncertainty

Uncertainty and complexity adds difficulty to decisions in software development. We introduce a game version that will ease your way to maximize the benefit.

You'll have to choose between 2 paper planes, each with different properties and rewards. This is a parallel to having 2 strategies in software development, where you have to choose one and pursue it. In both cases you are trying to get maximum reward.

We'll take you through a step by step process that will make you understand how several concepts can help you in making decisions.
