Rules:

• In order to demonstrate advantages of the decision-making techniques and value analysis, we decided to design a game, which simulates real-world decision process in paper plane construction.

• Main target for players is to choose the type of the plane and build in limited amount of time.

• In adition to that, players have to estimate cost (time it takes to build a plane), as well as potential benefit (based on eastimation of destance it will fly).

• Before game starts, players can look up the plane contruction steps and make predictions.
