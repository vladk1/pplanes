Paper Planes
     or decision making under uncertainty
The goal of this web page

The website gives users an opportunity to experience the use of cost-benefit analysis to perform decisions under uncertaity. By given a scenario of making paper planes, users follow the steps on the website which inputs estimation of different attributes. As result, the system will automatically return a graph that analysis the project portfolios. By looking at the result, users should be able to determine which portfolio is better under uncertainty.

Hopefully, users can have better understanding of making decision under uncertainty and how useful to use this technique during decision-making process.
