Rules of the game

The main objective of the game is to give example of application for decision-making process advantages through the game of constructing paper planes.

1) As you can see in the right, you have 2 types of planes from which you can choose. The Basic Dart has a value of 100 point associated, while The Stable has 300 points.

2) Comparing the instructions, you need to make a decision of which one you want to play.

3) Choose one type of the plane which will give the best benefit in terms of flight distance and the least amount of cost in terms of time it will take to produce.

4) Build the plane and measure your building time.

5) Throw a plane and measure flying distance to get overall score.

6) In the following, you’ll choose one of the planes, make it and fly it. Is it hard? Go to the next page to find out more how you can ease this process. Uncertainty Estimation
